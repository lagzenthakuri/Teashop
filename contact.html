Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary by location but generally take 3-7 business days within Europe and 7-14 days internationally.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for businesses. Please contact us directly with your business details and requirements for our wholesale pricing structure.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened products. For quality concerns with opened products, please contact our customer service team.

How should I store my tea?

For optimal freshness, store tea in a cool, dark place in an airtight container away from strong odors, moisture, and direct sunlight.
