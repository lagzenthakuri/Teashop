The essence of Chinese tea culture

The Art of Green Tea

Green tea is the most natural form of tea, made from Camellia sinensis leaves that undergo minimal oxidation. Unlike black tea, green tea is pan-fired or steamed shortly after harvesting to prevent oxidation, preserving its delicate flavors, natural antioxidants, and vibrant green color.

Key Characteristics

  • Minimal oxidation process
  • Light, delicate flavor profile
  • High in antioxidants (EGCG)
